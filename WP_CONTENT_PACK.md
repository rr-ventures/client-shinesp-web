**H1:** How Speech Therapy Works

### Body Content

Starting speech therapy can feel like a big step, especially if you're not sure what to expect. At Shine and Speak, I've designed the process to be simple, transparent, and family-centred from the very first call.

## Step 1: Book a Free Discovery Call

The first step is a free, no-obligation phone call. During this 15–20 minute chat, we'll talk about your concerns, your child's strengths, and what you're hoping to achieve. I'll answer any questions you have about the therapy process, funding options, and how sessions work.

You don't need a referral or diagnosis to book a discovery call. If you're unsure whether speech therapy is needed, this call is the perfect place to start.

## Step 2: Comprehensive Assessment

If we decide to move forward, I'll arrange a thorough [speech and language assessment](/speech-therapy-assessment-sydney-nsw). Assessments take place at your home, your child's school, or via telehealth — whichever setting is most comfortable and practical.

During the assessment, I use a combination of standardised tools and informal observation to build a complete picture of communication strengths and areas for support. For bilingual families, I can assess in both English and Mandarin to ensure your child's true abilities are captured accurately.

After the assessment, you'll receive a [detailed report](/speech-therapy-reports-sydney-nsw) with clear findings and practical recommendations.

## Step 3: Your Personalised Therapy Plan

Based on the assessment, I'll create a therapy plan tailored to your child's specific goals. We'll discuss what progress looks like, how often sessions should occur, and how to measure outcomes.

### What happens in a typical session?

Sessions are usually 45–60 minutes and are designed around your child's interests and real-world needs. I use play-based, evidence-based approaches — therapy should feel engaging, not like a chore.

Every session includes time for parent coaching. I'll show you strategies you can use between sessions so your child continues to make progress throughout the week.

### Where do sessions take place?

- **At home** — I travel to you with everything needed for a full session. [Learn about mobile therapy](/mobile-speech-therapy-sydney-nsw).
- **At school or kindergarten** — I visit your child's learning environment and can coordinate with teachers. [Explore school-based therapy](/school-based-speech-therapy-sydney-nsw).
- **Online** — [Telehealth sessions](/telehealth-speech-therapy-sydney-nsw) are available Australia-wide for families who prefer online delivery.

## Ongoing Review and Progress

Therapy isn't set-and-forget. I regularly review goals, track progress, and adjust the plan as your child develops. I also provide progress reports for NDIS plan reviews, schools, and other professionals involved in your child's care.

## Funding Options

I'm a registered [NDIS provider](/ndis-speech-therapy-sydney-nsw) (all management types) and a [Medicare provider](/medicare-speech-therapy-sydney-nsw). I also accept [private pay](/private-speech-therapy-sydney-nsw), [Home Care Packages](/home-care-package-speech-therapy-sydney-nsw), and [DVA funding](/dva-speech-therapy-sydney-nsw). Visit the [fees and pricing page](/fees) for current rates.
